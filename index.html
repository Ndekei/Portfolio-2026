About Me

Designing with

I'm Victor Ndekei — a Design Lead who believes that exceptional design is the intersection of empathy, strategy, and craft.

Currently leading design at Roamtech, I shape digital experiences that serve millions of users. My work spans the full spectrum — from deep user research and UX strategy to high-fidelity interfaces and design systems.

I'm simultaneously sharpening my business lens through an MBA at the University of Salford, ensuring every design decision I make is rooted in both user needs and business reality.
