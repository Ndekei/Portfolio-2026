Case Study

K24 Plus Mobile App

Leading the end-to-end Research and Design for a content aggregation platform that scaled to over 1 million users.

Overview

K24 Plus is a revolutionary Over the Top (OTT) content platform in Kenya that allows users to "only pay for what you like." It features premium Video on Demand (VOD) and live channels, catering to both free and pay-as-you-consume customers.

Project Role & Context

  • Role: UI/UX Design & Product Management
  • Context: Joined 6 months before launch. The product had been in development for two years with various hurdles before a dedicated team was assigned.
  • Responsibilities: Sole Product Designer in charge of Research, Design, and Development Relations.

Key Objectives

  • Complete the full product design.
  • Oversee development and ensure quality standards.
  • Manage Deployment, Testing, and Quality Assurance.
  • Assist marketing with promotional strategy and design.
  • Prioritized Features: Authentication, Content Aggregation, and M-Pesa Payment Integration.

Design Process

  • Initial Evaluation: Switched from an Adobe XD file to a new design framework.
  • Wireframes: Developed low-fidelity wireframes to align stakeholders on the basic model before high-fidelity design began.
  • Hi-Fidelity UI: Focused on a clean, content-first interface with features like "Play Now," "Watch Trailer," and integrated payment modals.

Outcomes

My experience at K24 involved navigating a live product environment, ensuring top-tier product quality by acting on user feedback, and balancing the roles of a designer and manager to deliver a market-leading OTT platform.
